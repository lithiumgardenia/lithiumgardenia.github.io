Black Mass & Raspberries

A dessert for the daring, decadent, and unrepentant.
Dark chocolate, espresso, and cream cheese converge in molten chaos, intertwined with tangy, ruby-red raspberry-strawberry lava. One bite and you’ll taste sin.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 box Great Value fudge brownie mix
  • ½ cup dark cocoa powder
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ tsp sea salt

Wet Ingredients

  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
  • 8 oz hot espresso
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 2 oz dark chocolate fudge sauce
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp raspberry extract (Watkin’s Extrait De Framboise)

Preserve Core

  • 6 oz strawberry preserves (or other fruit preserves)
  • 2 tsp raspberry extract
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease an 8×8 pan or line with parchment.
  2. Prepare the dry mix: Whisk together brownie mix, cocoa powder, sugar, and sea salt.
  3. Chocolate mousse binder: Blend cream cheese and chocolate fudge sauce until smooth.
  4. Combine wet ingredients: Mix melted coconut oil, hot espresso, vanilla, and raspberry extract.
  5. Create the batter: Fold wet ingredients into dry mix, then gently fold in cream cheese mousse to leave pockets of molten cream cheese.
  6. Prepare the preserve core: Mix strawberry preserves, raspberry extract, lemon juice, and sugar until smooth.
  7. Assemble: Pour batter into pan. Dollop preserve mixture unevenly and marble lightly.
  8. Bake: 325°F for 35–45 minutes. Edges set, center jiggles.
  9. Cool: 20–30 minutes. Brownies will firm slightly but remain molten.
  10. Serve: Slice with hot-water-dipped knife or spoon. Optional: fresh raspberries, sea salt, chocolate drizzle.

This produces a dense, fudgy, molten masterpiece, perfect for late-night rituals, decadent gatherings, or summoning chaos in your kitchen.
